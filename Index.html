01 — About

The Sound
of Experience

Karl Antoine is a pianist whose music exists at the intersection of soul, jazz, and cinematic emotion. Every performance is an invitation — to feel, to exhale, to be present in the moment the keys breathe life into a room.

"Music isn't something I play. It's something I live through my hands."

Whether holding down a weekly residency at the airport, leading worship in church sanctuaries, or commanding the late-night energy of lounges and bars with his full band — vocalist, bassist, and drummer — Karl brings an undeniable presence to every stage.
